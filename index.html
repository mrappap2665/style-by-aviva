Meet Aviva

The best interior designer is nature — and rooms should feel just as alive.

Aviva is a New Jersey–based interior designer and corporate stylist with a simple belief: your home should put the “U” in “HOUSE.” Her spaces showcase your personal treasures and collections, layer warm color and texture, and always leave room for a touch of the garden.

Beyond full design projects, she brings a maker’s hand that most firms can’t — hand-dried florals grown in her own garden, custom-painted furniture transformations, and styled tablescapes that turn a dinner into an occasion.

The Process

Clear, collaborative, creative

Step One

Discover

We meet, walk the space, and talk about how you live or work — your style, timeline, and budget.

Step Two

Design

Concepts, color stories, and layouts come together into a cohesive vision for your space.

Step Three

Curate

Every piece is sourced or handmade — furniture, lighting, art, florals, and the details in between.

Step Four

Reveal

Installation day: the space is styled down to the last stem, ready for you to simply enjoy.
