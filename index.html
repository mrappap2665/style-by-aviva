The Process

Clear, collaborative, and designed to make the experience as beautiful as the result.

Step One

Discover

We meet, walk the space, and talk about how you live or work, your style, timeline, and budget.

Step Two

Design

Concepts, palettes, layouts, and selections come together into a cohesive vision for your space.

Step Three

Curate

Every piece is sourced and ordered — furniture, lighting, art, and the details that make it yours.

Step Four

Reveal

Installation day: the space is styled down to the last stem, ready for you to simply enjoy it.
